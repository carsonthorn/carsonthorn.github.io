About me.

Carson Thorn, Freelance Illustrator and Comic Artist.

Born and raised in central Kentucky, I grew up fascinated by the works of American and Japanese artists alike. Emboldened by their influence, I taught myself how to draw in hopes to make art that would inspire others to be creative. By the end of high school I was participating and placing in various comic award contests across Japan.
